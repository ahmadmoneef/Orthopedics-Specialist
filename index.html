about us

the introduction

The International Hospital is one of the largest private hospitals in the State of Kuwait, which is strategically located in the heart of the Salmiya area on Qatar Street, and is designed with the latest international standards. The hospital provides all medical services in terms of diagnosis and treatment, as it includes a group of the most skilled doctors in all different specialties.

The hospital is characterized by the presence of a distinguished group of qualified and trained customer service staff to complete patient transactions in a smooth and fast manner, and the level of hotel services provided to patients is of high quality and technology.
